About Page

UNEARTHING GREAT IDEAS

Print, moving images, online & outdoor

The Creative Mine is a multi-disciplined creative agency skilled in all media including online, outdoor, versatile video content and TV. Count on us for everything from strategies to straplines, from a brand refresh to a brand new website.

Our name is a tribute to our founder’s ancestor Thomas Bowskill. 250 years after he was Master Colliery of Hornby Castle, England, his direct descendant Angela Bowskill is digging up something else of great value - ideas.
